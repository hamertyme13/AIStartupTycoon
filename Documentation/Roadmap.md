# Roadmap

## Current Focus

The current priority is turning the game from a functional prototype into an App Store-ready tycoon experience.

## Completed Foundations

- Core dashboard and Rogue Circuit visual theme.
- Opening scene.
- Products, employees, investors, research, AI models, offices, and competitors.
- Monthly reports and notifications.
- Multiple victory routes.
- Research costs for player and competitors.
- Employee career paths and randomized avatars.
- Customer satisfaction recovery through support capacity and customer success sprints.
- Save schema versioning.
- Initial App Store icon asset.
- Launch checklist for first-session onboarding.
- Contextual news and monthly pulse events.
- In-app TestFlight hub with beta focus, support, and privacy links.
- TestFlight QA checklist.
- iOS deployment target lowered for broader beta coverage.

## Near-Term Gameplay

- Add more player-authored scenarios with unique modifiers.
- Add acquisition offers as interactive choices before final victory.
- Add IPO preparation steps: audit, compliance, banker selection, roadshow, and pricing.
- Add board/investor pressure events tied to runway, growth, and dilution.
- Add employee morale and burnout as explicit metrics.
- Add product quality upgrades and technical-debt decisions.
- Add customer segments: consumers, startups, enterprise, government, and developers.
- Add more competitor personalities and visible rivalry arcs.

## Make The Game Feel More Alive

- More monthly storylets that react to company metrics.
- Recurring rival behavior that players can recognize and anticipate.
- Named enterprise customers and contract renewals.
- Founder mail or board memos after major milestones.
- Seasonal market cycles and AI hype waves.
- Press headlines tied to player choices.
- Rare breakthrough and scandal events.

## App Store Readiness

- Publish final privacy policy and support pages.
- Create final screenshots for required iPhone sizes.
- Run the TestFlight QA checklist before each upload.
- Add crash/logging review strategy.
- Review accessibility: Dynamic Type, VoiceOver labels, contrast, Reduce Motion, and tap targets.
- Add App Store metadata: subtitle, keywords, promotional text, and description.
- Decide monetization before review: paid app, free with IAP, or premium expansion.

## Later Possibilities

- iCloud save sync.
- Game Center achievements.
- Daily challenge scenario.
- Offline analytics for balancing.
- Expansion packs for different startup eras.
- Sandbox mode with customizable starting conditions.
- Web companion page on Rogue Circuit.
